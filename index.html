About LeBron

LeBron James is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Born in Akron, Ohio, he was drafted as the #1 pick in 2003 and has since become a four-time NBA Champion, four-time MVP, and the league’s all-time leading scorer.

Off the court, LeBron is a philanthropist, entrepreneur, and advocate for education and social justice. His I PROMISE School and the LeBron James Family Foundation have changed thousands of lives in his hometown and beyond.
